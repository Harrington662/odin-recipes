Strawberry Bellini

Description

A strawberry bellini is a nice drink for Mother's Day, a brunch, or a shower.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups strawberries, hulled & sliced
  • 1/4 cup confectioners' sugar
  • 1 tbsp brandy
  • 1.5 cups chilled sparkling wine
  • 3 large whole strawberries

Steps

  1. Blend hulled strawberries, confectioners' sugar, and brandy in a blender until smooth.
  2. Chill in the refrigerator for 10 minutes.
  3. Divide the strawberry mixture among three champagne flutes. Pour 1/2 cup sparkling wine into each flute and stir to combine.
  4. Garnish each glass with a whole strawberry.
